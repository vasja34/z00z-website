## Page Brief

What
: Website privacy policy and service-layer data handling, distinct from protocol privacy claims.

When
: Used when deploying analytics, forms, support tickets, or community integrations.

Where
: Footer.

Who
: Site users, counsel, support teams, and maintainers.

Why
: Website data collection is a separate privacy surface from protocol settlement privacy.

How
: Describe collected data, forms, cookies, analytics, support data, retention, user choices, and protocol/privacy distinction.
