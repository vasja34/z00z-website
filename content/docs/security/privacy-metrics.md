## Page Brief

What
: Privacy quality signals such as ingress/egress posture, star and collector patterns, remix depth, and telemetry boundaries.

When
: Used by wallet designers, operators, and researchers measuring privacy quality.

Where
: Security and Protocol privacy.

Who
: Privacy researchers, wallet teams, data teams, and operators.

Why
: Metrics can guide improvement, but must not become consensus truth or a fake universal score.

How
: Define metric families, collection limits, local-first telemetry, aggregation rules, anti-pattern warnings, and non-goals.
