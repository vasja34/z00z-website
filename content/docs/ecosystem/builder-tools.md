## Page Brief

What
: Curated registry of official, experimental, and third-party tools for building, testing, indexing, verifying, or operating around Z00Z.

When
: Used after a builder knows what they want to build and needs concrete tools or integrations.

Where
: Ecosystem builders, Developers, Support, and footer utility links.

Who
: Tool authors, wallet teams, operators, auditors, indexer builders, and partner developers.

Why
: Hyperliquid's builder-tool pattern is useful, but Z00Z must clearly separate official tools, community tools, and non-endorsed experiments.

How
: Group tools by official status, role, maturity, maintainer, repo link, security notes, verification command, last-reviewed date, and a non-endorsement disclaimer for third-party listings.
