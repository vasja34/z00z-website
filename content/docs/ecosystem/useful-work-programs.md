## Page Brief

What
: Contributor programs for bounded grants, bounties, audits, research, documentation, operations, and agent tasks.

When
: Used once treasury or ecosystem programs are ready to announce scoped work.

Where
: Ecosystem and Governance.

Who
: Contributors, reviewers, program managers, treasury participants, and auditors.

Why
: Rewards must be verifiable, challengeable, and private at payout rather than discretionary.

How
: Publish work categories, WorkPackage requirements, review lanes, reward bands, challenge windows, and non-employment terms.
