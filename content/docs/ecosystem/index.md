## Section Lens

Source
: marketing strategy, useful-work paper, tokenomics, legal architecture, repo tools, and participation docs.

Message
: participation must be qualified by role, maturity, evidence, and non-endorsement boundaries.

UX
: a participation hub with directories, badges, review metadata, and clear calls to the right next page.

Include
: role-specific entry points, intake criteria, official-vs-third-party status, program scope, and support handoffs.

Avoid
: endorsement ambiguity, hype framing, unreviewed tool promotion, or community links that replace support.
